About Me

Hello! I'm Sophie, the face behind Turn Up Turn Down. I moved to Waihi at the start of 2025 with my partner Jon. Originally from Te Awamutu, I'm a small-town girl at heart and absolutely love being part of close-knit communities.

By trade, I'm an anaesthetic technician and still contract in Auckland when needed—but Waihi is now home. When I'm not working, you'll find me playing a bit of tennis or playing in the Matamata Brass Band.

Turn Up Turn Down was born from my love of helping people and my appreciation for a well-prepared, stress-free holiday. I'm here to take care of the little things so you can focus on enjoying your time away. I'm excited to meet more of the amazing locals and bach owners in the area and look forward to helping make your time at the beach effortless and welcoming.
